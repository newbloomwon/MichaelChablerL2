# Project Ghost - Design Document

## 1. The Core Problem
In 2026, the average person’s digital footprint is no longer just a "list of sites." It is a weaponized data profile used by:
*   **Data Brokers:** Selling home addresses and phone numbers for profit.
*   **AI Scrapers:** Training models on private social media content without consent.
*   **Bad Actors:** Using public "metadata" to craft perfect phishing attacks.
*   **The "Time Tax":** Most users care about privacy but lack the 40+ hours required to manually hunt and delete their data from 200+ broker sites.

## 2. The Solution: "The Ghost Engine"
A mobile-first application that functions as a Personal Privacy Proxy. It doesn't just "show" you the problem; it fixes it autonomously.

### Key Features
*   **The Footprint Scan:** An instant "Deep Web" audit that finds the user's name, address, relatives, and exposed photos across 400+ data broker databases (e.g., BeenVerified, Acxiom, Whitepages).
*   **Agentic Deletion (The "Killer Strike"):** The app acts as a Legal Proxy (via Limited Power of Attorney). With one tap, the AI files official CCPA/GDPR "Right to be Forgotten" requests on the user's behalf.
*   **Leak Alerts:** Real-time notifications when a new "Shadow Profile" appears or when the user's data resurfaces after a previous deletion.
*   **Social Scrubbing:** An AI tool that scans old social media posts for sensitive content (geotags, personal details) and suggests bulk-archiving or deletion.

## 3. Market Opportunity & Targeting
The "Data Removal" market in 2026 is shifting toward Automated Personal Security.

### Primary Targets
*   **The "Time-Poor" Professional:** Executives and lawyers who need privacy but can't manage it themselves.
*   **Public Figures & VIPs:** Targeted individuals (creators, journalists) at high risk of "doxing."
*   **The "Ghost" Tier (Privacy Enthusiasts):** Users who want a $0$ digital footprint and are willing to pay for continuous monitoring.

## 4. Business Model (Monetization)
To avoid "Subscription Fatigue" in 2026, the app will use a Hybrid Value Model:
*   **Freemium Scan:** Free "Privacy Health Check" to show the user exactly where they are exposed (the "Hook").
*   **The Cleanse (One-Time):** A $29.99$ one-time fee for a "Bulk Wipe" of all found data.
*   **Ghost Mode (Subscription):** $9.99/mo$ for ongoing "Guard Duty"—preventing data from ever being re-listed and providing Dark Web monitoring.

## 5. Strategic "Killer Strike"
**The Goal:** Prove the value within 60 seconds of download.

**The "Magic Moment":** Upon opening the app, the user enters their email. The app immediately displays a Map of 50+ data brokers that currently have their home address for sale. Below the map is a single button: "Go Ghost." This creates an immediate "Loss Aversion" psychological trigger—the user sees what they've lost (privacy) and is offered an instant, one-click solution.
