# Project "Ghost" (Michael’s Idea)

## Vision
Empower everyday users to reclaim their digital identity by moving from a state of Total Exposure to Strategic Invisibility.

## Executive Summary
In today's digital landscape, users face an increasingly high level of exposure of their private/personal information and personal content. Private corporations and general public actors look to extract and capitalize on their online material. 

"Ghost" offers a medium through which users can be alerted of digital spaces where their footprint (essentially their digital fingerprint) is in danger of being exposed or compromised. It serves as a tool through which this can be limited and heavily reduced.

This primarily targets people who are concerned with their privacy but don’t have the time to perform the necessary due diligence on every digital space.
